Kia

Engineering Intern • Summer 2024

Overview

As an Engineering Intern at Kia, I contributed to automotive engineering projects focused on vehicle development and testing. This experience provided valuable exposure to large-scale manufacturing processes and automotive industry standards.

Key Responsibilities

  • Supported vehicle development and testing initiatives
  • Collaborated with cross-functional engineering teams
  • Participated in design reviews and technical meetings
  • Conducted analysis and documentation of engineering processes

Technical Skills & Tools

Throughout this internship, I gained experience with:

  • Automotive engineering principles and practices
  • Manufacturing processes and quality control
  • Technical documentation and reporting
  • Cross-functional team collaboration
  • Industry-standard engineering tools and software

Impact & Learnings

This internship provided invaluable insight into the automotive industry's engineering practices. Working with a major automotive manufacturer taught me the importance of rigorous testing, quality standards, and the complex coordination required to bring vehicles from concept to production.

I learned how large-scale engineering teams operate, the critical role of documentation and standardization, and how design decisions impact manufacturing efficiency and product quality.
